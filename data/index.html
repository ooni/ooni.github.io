OONI Explorer is the world's largest open dataset on internet censorship, consisting of millions of measurements collected from more than 200 countries since 2012.

The OONI API enables you to perform your own analysis of OONI data. For batch consumption of OONI data, you can setup your own instance of the OONI PostgreSQL MetaDB or fetch the whole OONI dataset from the ooni-data Amazon S3 bucket.
