Join us on Slack or IRC to participate in community discussions. Sign-up for the ooni-talk mailing list to receive updates and discuss all things related to the OONI-verse.

All OONI source code is available on GitHub, where we also document issues and future plans.

We also have a growing list of OONI ambassadors through our partnership program.
