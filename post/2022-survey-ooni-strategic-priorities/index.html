Survey: Help shape OONI’s strategic priorities

Maria Xynou 2022-10-20

OONI will celebrate its 10th anniversary on 5th December 2022!

This is a time for celebration, but also a time for reflection: What worked well? What should we improve? What should we do differently going forward?

The OONI community has been at the heart of our work over the past decade, and so we invite our community to help shape OONI’s strategic priorities for the future.

Please take a few minutes to complete our survey: https://ooni.typeform.com/2022-survey

We’d appreciate it if you could please submit your survey responses by Sunday, 20th November 2022.

If you’d also like to participate in a recorded interview to share your OONI community experience, please fill out the survey (while sharing your email address through the last survey question) by Sunday, 6th November 2022.

Thanks for your time, your feedback is very valuable to us. Thanks for helping to shape OONI’s goals for the future!
