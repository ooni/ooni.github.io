OONI at the CryptoRave 2017

Vasilis Ververis 2017-05-12

CryptoRave is a free, non-commercial and collectively funded 48 hour event featuring talks, workshops and activities related to security, privacy, anonymity, hacking and freedom of expression that attracts more that 2,500 people mainly from South America. The event took place in São Paulo, Brazil on 5th and 6th May 2017.

A number of people attended the OONI presentation and workshop (“Listatona”) that was co-organized with Coding Rights. During the workshop participants were able to better understand OONI’s testing methodology, and how OONI Probe performs network measurements. Participants provided useful feedback regarding test-lists and URLs from diverse sources from South America.
